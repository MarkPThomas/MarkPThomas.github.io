mark-p-thomas : Track Graph Control/Display

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Feb 09, 2024

Graphs have not been implemented as of the MVP release, but the control and placeholders have all been created in order to display tracks.

In v1, this will be available to show tracks as functions of distance OR time. Graphs will also be able to show track stats as histograms in order to better assess the validity of the higher order stats, such as standard deviations.
