mark-p-thomas : GIS

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Nov 03, 2023

GIS is a one-stop shop for GIS work, such as generating layers for Leaflet, CRUD and auto-cleaning features for routes & tracks. It is made to be extensible, so you can derive your own custom objects and methods of manipulating & cleaning them.

GIS is the main model of GIS.ly. Nearly all of the logic in GIS.ly is performed through this library & itâ€™s dependencies. For example, GPX & KML files are read into a GeoJSON object, which is loaded into GIS as a GeoJSON object & mirrored as a Polyline for further data derivations & manipulations.
