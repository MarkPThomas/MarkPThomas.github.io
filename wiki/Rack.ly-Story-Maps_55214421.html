mark-p-thomas : Rack.ly Story Maps

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Nov 04, 2023

Story maps are 2-dimensional maps, in which major user activities are arrayed along the horizontal dimension, loosely ordered by time left->right, with tasks aligned along a vertical axis. These maps help in adding more detail to user stories in order to work out different features & processes for development.

See my Confluence page for more information on Story Maps.
