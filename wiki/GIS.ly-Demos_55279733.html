Introduction

GIS.ly is an app that makes editing GPS tracks fast & easy. Most of this involves clearing out bad or undesired data recorded by a GPS device. Additional context & data are displayed as well in order better help a user decide on what to edit, how, and if results are as desired. The animation below quickly showcases the main usages of the app in one session.

For a detailed breakdown of app functionality, see the How-To section.

Child Pages

These contain particular scenarios with descriptions, relevant links to problem descriptions & solutions, as well as animations showing an example of the app addressing the issue.

  • GPS Signal Lost & Regained During Recording
  • Recording Includes Time Not in Activity
  • Multiple Days Included in Single Recording
  • Recording Includes Car Ride
  • Recording Has Many Stops/Breaks
  • Recorded Points Inaccurate at Times of Poor GPS Data
  • Recordings Have Long Breaks in Areas of Poor Reception
  • Recording Missed Sections of the Route
