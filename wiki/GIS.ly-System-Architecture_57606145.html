mark-p-thomas : GIS.ly System Architecture

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Nov 18, 2023

While GIS.ly at first glance appears to just be a React-Leaflet component that displays GPS Tracks by adding GeoJSON objects to app state, there is more going on under the hood to assist in optimized speed & organized complexity.

For deeper information specific to the underlying GIS library, see the GIS System Architecture section.

Interactive diagrams can be found at FigJam.
