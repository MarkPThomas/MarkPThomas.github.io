mark-p-thomas : Polyline

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Nov 08, 2023

A polyline is a continuous line composed of one or more line segments. It is a standalone geometry that may also form a polygon/shape.

This section covers the development and behavior of the Polyline data structure, which is used to model polylines.
