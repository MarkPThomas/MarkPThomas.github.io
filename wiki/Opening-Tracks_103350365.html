Loading Tracks

First Load

The app operates out of your browser, so you can directly load any supported file that you have locally on your computer or phone.

Currently supported formats are: *.gpx, & *.kml

Second Load

Currently, the app adds additional tracks to the session, but at this point the MVP is not reliable with Undo/Redo history or appropriate Comparison Stats.

In v1, users will either be limited to a default situation (hinted at in the modal shown), or this edge case will be handled to show all contingencies properly.
