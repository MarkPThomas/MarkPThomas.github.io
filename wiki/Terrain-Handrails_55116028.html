Introduction

Taken from an orienteering technique, handrails are continuous features that you can reliably intersect and then follow to work out where you are or help you get to a destination with less error & effort.

For offsets & just plain noise, GIS.ly v1 will use the handrail orienteering technique as inspiration for correcting a track. The user merely needs to select the start & end vertices, whether the technique is to follow a trough, crest, contour, or most likely viable/possible movement, and the app will attempt to find a route between these two points following the terrain data using the elevation API with some statistical reasoning, calculus, & backtracking techniques.
