Introduction

Splitting by multiple activities is much more involved than for a single activity. The following is an example case of this being done. The situation is one where a triathlete does a warmup walk in the morning, drives to their competition, competes in the 3 events, & then drives home before turning off their GPS device. For most people, the recorded data is pretty useless. Although GPX files CAN be manually edited by those with enough knowledge in a text editor, that is not a feasible remedy.
