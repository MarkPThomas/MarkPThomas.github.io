mark-p-thomas : Recordings Have Long Breaks in Areas of Poor Reception

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Mar 21, 2024

An additional case of meaningless Points can come from longer breaks in areas with bad satellite reception. In these cases, the GPS recording may indicate movement by recording inaccurate points. I am calling these noise clouds. Choosing Clean Operations > Smooth > Noise Clouds can remove such Points, leaving a first-stopped and first-resumed point in order to maintain information as to the length of the break while stripping out the incorrect ‘movement’.

Noise clouds are needing further refinement before a better demonstration can be shown. I will add images/animations here once this feature is fully operational.
