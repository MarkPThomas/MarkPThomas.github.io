mark-p-thomas : GIS.ly Controls & Displays

Created by Mark Thomas, last modified on Mar 20, 2024

Controls are interactive elements in the app that perform actions on Tracks, settings, displays, etc. Displays just display information, although some interactivity may be possible. Either of these may conditionally appear depending on the state of the session.

Above is the basic control layout when the app first loads. Below is the expanded layout after loading a Track for editing.
